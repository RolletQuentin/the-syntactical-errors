What is the project ?

For the Python Discord code jam, we have to make a Python project about the image manipulation and one theme which is secret codes.

Our team decides to put sound in your ears. With our tool, you can encode a song in a picture and send it to your friends ! Your friend can then put the picture in the decoder and then listening the music.
